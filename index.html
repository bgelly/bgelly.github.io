FAQ

Drivers

How much can I earn?

Drivers can earn up to $150 per month and are paid by the number of passenger impressions generated each week. As Rideo continues to expand, so will driver earnings.

What do I have to do?

Driving for Rideo is easy. Just attach a tablet with Rideo’s app installed to the back of the passenger side headrest with a removable strap to display content. We can provide the tablet, headrest, charger and all other gear you need if you do not have it already, and over a three-month payment plan drivers can use Rideo earnings to reimburse us for the cost.

What will be in my car?

Each Rideo driver has a 10-inch tablet attached to the back of the passenger side headrest with a removable strap. The tablet has a power cord and drivers must be able to plug it into a power source. The tablet displays, video, audio and full-frame static ads.

How do I get paid and how often?

Drivers are paid each week to their paypal or venmo account.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out this form and we will be in touch personally within 24 hours.

Will my rideshare account be deactivated or will I be fined for using Rideo?

No. Uber has explicitly said that Rideo is not against their policy and they do not penalize drivers for advertising. Rideo guarantees payment of all fines directly related to our service up to $50.

Do I need to buy a tablet?

Rideo provides a tablet, headrest, charger and all necessary other gear you need if you do not have it already. Drivers can use Rideo earnings to reimburse us for the cost over a three-month payment plan of $50.32 per month. ($119.99 for the tablet, $18.80 for the holder, $8.50 for safety and power cord, $3.66 for screen cover)

How does tipping work?

Rideo’s tablet prompts passengers to tip drivers. 100% of tips are distributed weekly to drivers in addition to Rideo earnings.

Is Rideo available in my city?

Rideo is currently available in the greater New York and New Jersey metropolitan area. If you’re interested in driving with us but not working within our current operating zones, don’t worry! We’re expanding rapidly and plan to come to your city very soon.

What happens if my tablet is damaged or stolen by a customer?

Rideo drivers each have a power cord and strap to prevent passenger theft. Each tablet has a protective cover to prevent damage. Rideo is not liable for passenger theft or damage.

Do I have to sign a contract to use Rideo for a certain amount of time?

Drivers are not obligated to drive for Rideo for any period of time or hours per week. We will always buy back equipment and tablet within the first three months from drivers.

What do I get for referring a business or driver?

Rideo drivers who sign up a driver or business receive $10 per driver referral and $150 per business referral.

Advertisers

How does Rideo advertising work?

Through Rideo, businesses can display advertisements in rideshare vehicles such as Uber and Lyft. The ads are displayed on interactive 10-inch tablets through various mediums, including videos, games, banners and full-frame static ads. Ads are interspersed and combined with other content to ensure passengers engage with the tablet during their ride. Learn More

How much does Rideo charge?

Our starter plan is $39.95 for 1000 views in the form of video or stillframe advertisements. Find out more here. We charge as little as $0.02 per view for larger orders.

How many people will see my ad?

That’s up to you! Our plans range from 1,000 to 19,000+ views.

How many drivers do you have?

Rideo aims to have 100+ active drivers by the end of 2017.

What types of ads are played on Rideo?

Advertisers can display content across video, banner or full-frame static.

How are customer impressions calculated?

Rideo calculates impressions by tracking driver trips and recording the number of times your ad is displayed.

Can a customer mute my ad?

Passengers can lower the volume of the tablet to a level that is only audible to those intentionally listening, but they cannot silence it.

How will my ad be presented in a rideshare car?

Rideo ads are displayed on a 10-inch tablet on the back of the passenger side headrest. When customers click on your ad to find out more they will be redirected to an interactive map featuring your location, your website, or any other page/content of your choosing. Advertisements are interspersed with other content to ensure passenger engagement.

How long is the commitment and can I cancel my monthly subscription?

There is no commitment beyond one month. Rideo advertising partners can cancel at any time.

Is Rideo available in my city?

Rideo is currently available in the greater New York and New Jersey metropolitan area. If you’re interested in advertising with us outside of this region, don’t worry! We’re expanding rapidly.

What if I don’t have premade ad content?

Rideo is happy to provide advertisements for our partners. We have an experienced team of designers on hand to get your advertisement up and running. Whether you choose to provide your own advertisements or have Rideo create one for you, we’ll work with you to make sure you’re happy with the campaign you’re launching.

What do I get for referring another business?

Rideo advertisers get three months of free advertising for referring another business that signs up for our service.
